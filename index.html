The iris dataset

The iris dataset is a collection of measurements gathered from 50 flowers of 3 different species.

This dataset is bundled with R, and can be examined with the iris variable.

The following variables are available in the dataframe:

names(iris)

## [1] "Sepal.Length" "Sepal.Width"  "Petal.Length" "Petal.Width" 
## [5] "Species"
